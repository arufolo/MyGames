The Life of Greg - A game created in the 48 hour 2014 Global Game Jam at the UNC-Charlotte Location

TEAM WEBSITE: http://globalgamejam.org/2014/games/life-greg

To play the game, open 'The Life of Greg Upside Down Bird.html' file, be sure to have the Unity Web Player installed
OR access this link: 
http://www.upsidedownbird.com/games/the-life-of-greg/ 

You can view all the project's source (Including Scripts) in the Assets folder

You can access all of my individual scripts and assets by opening the 'Allen' folder inside of Assets 

**NOTE**
You can skip scenes by pressing SHIFT+T. The Scenes that I created are Lunch and the Work scene after Lunch.
